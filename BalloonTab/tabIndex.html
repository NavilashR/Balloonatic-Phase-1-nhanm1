About Us

About Balloon Asylum Helping others create unforgettable moments is what we live for. That's why our team of party scientists create innovative products, brainstorm wild party ideas, and meticulously track trendy trends (unicorns are sooo IN right now). It doesn't matter if you're celebrating taco Tuesday or your grandma's 100th birthday, every party deserves to be extraordinary, especially when it comes to the Halloween season. As far as we're concerned, Halloween isn't just one night. Oh, no no no. It's a season-long, orange and black spectacle. It's trips to the pumpkin patch, decorating, pumpkin carving, picking the perfect costume, picking another perfect costume (just in case), more decorating, trick-or-treating, costume contests, and throwing a petrifying party. We firmly believe that you're never too old to dress up and that being unapologetically unique is what we should all strive for. Sure, you could be a nurse. Or, you can take it to the next level by being a bearded zombie nurse from the 70s. Just sayin'. Whether you're dressing to impress or decorating to distress, be unforgettable. With hundreds of stores filled with thousands of products across the United States, you're sure to find the perfect costume, party theme, or inspiration for your next party.
